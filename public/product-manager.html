How to use this:
1. Check each category — edit the name or description if needed
2. Check each product — edit the name, price, or badge
3. Upload photos — tap "Upload Photo" on each product to add an image (use the best quality you have)
4. Click "+ Add Product" to add new products
5. Click "Remove" on products you want to take off the site
6. Choose your 6 Featured Products for the homepage
7. Click "Download Everything" at the bottom — this creates a ZIP with the product list + all your photos. Send it to Ibby!
